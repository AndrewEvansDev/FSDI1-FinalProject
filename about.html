Our Team

Lower Columbia Web Design is a local company recently created in 2021 meant to serve the needs of Cowlitz County. Its founder, Andrew Evans, has lived in Kelso for 6 years and has become a staple within the community, participating in various local events and organizations within the community.

Andrew is a strong advocate of all things local, purchasing from locally owned companies and giving back to the community. In 2020, due to ever increasing demands, it became to apparent to Andrew that Cowlitz County needed a tech sector. With that in mind, he reached out to a friend, who also happened to be a professor at SDGKU, and decided to pursue a career in technology. He started Lower Columbia Web Design to help the community achieve greater independance from the big cities nearby in their technology & development needs. In the future he intends on holding workshops to help highschool students gain and interest in the technology sector.

Andrew is a recent graduate of SDGKU's Full Stack Developer program and he brings to bear the skills of and experiences of many before him, while also utilizing talent from the world over. Counting among his teammates are developers from across the country and around the world, many with decades of experience who have worked for fortune 500 companies.
